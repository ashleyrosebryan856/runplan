Installation
On your phone
in 3 steps
01
Open in Safari
On your iPhone, open Safari (not Chrome) and go to runplan.app
02
Tap Share
Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up.
03
Add to Home Screen
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" then tap Add. RUNPLAN appears on your home screen like any app.
